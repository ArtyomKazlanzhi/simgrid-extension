# RaceStand — Product Summary

## Vision

Empower racers to instantly understand their championship position and calculate exactly what they need to win — eliminating manual spreadsheet calculations and giving them confidence in their race strategy.

## Target Audience

Sim racers and amateur motorsport competitors participating in point-based championships with multiple rounds.

## Core Features

- **Custom Scoring Table** — Define position-to-points mapping for any championship format
- **Fastest Lap Bonus** — Optional bonus points for fastest lap
- **Drop Rounds** — "Best X of Y" results calculation
- **Scenario Simulator** — Interactive "what if" tool for testing race outcomes

## Platform

Web application (browser-based, responsive)

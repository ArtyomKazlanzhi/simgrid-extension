SimGrid to RaceStand Importer

Import championship standings from TheSimGrid.com into RaceStand — the sim-racing championship tracker.

Features:
• One-click import of championship standings from any TheSimGrid championship page
• Automatically detects rounds, drivers, and points
• Opens results directly in RaceStand with data pre-filled
• Supports multi-class championships
• Works with both thesimgrid.com and www.thesimgrid.com

How to use:
1. Navigate to any championship page on TheSimGrid.com
2. Click the "Import to RaceStand" button that appears on the page
3. Review the imported data in the RaceStand viewer that opens

No account required. No data leaves your browser.
